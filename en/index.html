Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most frequently asked questions about our services

What are the cleaning service prices?

We offer competitive prices starting from 100 QAR for regular cleaning, varying based on space size and service type. Contact us for a free detailed quote.

Are cleaning materials and pesticides safe for families?

Yes, we only use licensed cleaning materials and pesticides approved by Qatar's Ministry of Municipality and Environment, internationally certified as safe for humans and pets.

Do you serve all areas in Qatar?

Yes, we cover all areas of Qatar including Doha, Al Rayyan, Al Wakra, Lusail, Al Khor, Umm Salal. Our mobile team reaches you within hours.

How long does it take to clean a villa or house?

It depends on size and condition, but typically cleaning an average villa takes 4 to 8 hours. We provide sufficient staff to ensure fast completion with highest quality.

Can I book the service via WhatsApp?

Absolutely! You can easily book via WhatsApp on 77175355, direct call, or through our website. We respond to all inquiries within minutes.
